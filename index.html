Thanks for visiting my webpage.

I am a PhD student in the Institute for Software Research (ISR) at Carnegie Mellon University, where I am advised by Prof. Jim Herbsleb.

My research involves studying large scale distributed collaborations in software development. I use a broad spectrum of techniques in my research, which include both qualitative and quantitative. Some of the topics that interest me are social network analysis, computational modeling, multi-agent systems and applied machine learning.
